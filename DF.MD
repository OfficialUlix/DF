EP: Dead Frequencies

TL;DR theme:
A world — and a mind — slowly breaking down like a failing signal. Systems collapse, connection is lost, everything falls into silence… but something human refuses to disappear.

⸻

Story Arc

The EP follows a metaphorical collapse of both society and the self, told through the language of signals, circuits, static, and decay.

1. Static Signals
The first glitches appear. The world feels off — systems flicker, structures show cracks, something beneath the surface is failing.

2. Drown Tonight
The collapse becomes personal. The noise overwhelms the protagonist emotionally, feeling like they’re drowning in pressure and disconnection.

3. Ghost
The protagonist disconnects from themselves.
They’re still physically present — but mentally absent.
Identity fractures. They become a “ghost” inside their own life.

4. Neon Graves
Total breakdown. The city becomes a graveyard of dead lights and lost signals — everything that once felt stable is gone.

5. Light The Fire 
A moment of defiance. Instead of waiting for the system to fix itself, the protagonist breaks free from it.

6. We Remain
After the collapse, silence settles. The world is scarred and broken, but something survives in the ruins.

⸻

Core Message

Even when everything fails —
systems, connections, identity, stability —
the human signal doesn’t disappear.

It survives the noise.

It survives the silence.
